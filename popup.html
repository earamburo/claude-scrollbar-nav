🗺️ Minimap Navigator

Visual scrollbar with clickable flags for instant navigation through long Claude conversations.

Minimap appears on the right side
🎯 Click to Jump
Click any flag marker to instantly jump to that conversation section
👆 Drag to Scroll
Drag the viewport indicator to smoothly scroll through the conversation
💬 Hover Preview
Hover near markers to see a tooltip with the prompt text
Toggle Minimap Ctrl+Shift+M
Next Section Ctrl+Shift+N
Previous Section Ctrl+Shift+P

The minimap updates automatically as you continue your conversation.
